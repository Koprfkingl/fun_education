health time cycles

Purpose of the app is provide a timer for multiple health cycles
defined by user.

When sitting for more than one hour in the same
position, negative health impacts may occur. When this becomes
a habit, risk of medical conditions incereases. Therefore it is
recommended to stretch the body after sitting for an hour.

Also when medical conditions like limited movement ability or back
sore already occured, it is recommended to stretch body regularly.

Very important is drinking habit (not AA). Drink a sip of water
every 15 minutes. This way body should stay hydrated.
